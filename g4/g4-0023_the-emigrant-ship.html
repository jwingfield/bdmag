The Emigrant Ship

Artist: Charles Joseph Staniland

Created:

Crowded genre scenes were immensely popular with the Victorian public and frequently mass-produced as prints for middle class homes. The narrative detail in this painting is the key to its popularity - each group of figures has a story to tell. \n Staniland's skill as a draughtsman is seen in the composition of this picture. The gazes and gestures of the people on the shore waving farewell lead the eye towards those on board the ship itself.
